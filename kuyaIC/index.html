Artist Statement

My practice sits between graphic design and fine art. I explore structure, negative space, and emotional tension through visual form. Each project is approached as both a conceptual and aesthetic inquiry.

I work across print, digital media, and physical materials, allowing the medium to influence the final outcome. My work is influenced by modernist design, contemporary art, and editorial composition.
