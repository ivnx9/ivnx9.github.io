Welcome to SmartHydroFarm
From research innovation to smart agriculture technology.
SmartHydroFarm is an IoT-powered smart agriculture solution designed for automated vertical hydroponics. It monitors pH, TDS/EC, temperature, humidity, and water level while supporting remote access, automation, and AI-assisted crop management.
SmartHydroFarm
IoT Monitoring • Automation • AI Support
Originally developed as an academic research project, SmartHydroFarm is now evolving into a scalable smart farming platform for hydroponic growers, students, and agriculture innovators.
